This page is dedicated to Formula 1 racing with the Red Bull Racing team.

About Red Bull Racing

Red Bull Racing is a Formula One racing team, racing under an Austrian license, based in the United Kingdom. The team has been one of the frontrunners in Formula 1 since its inception in 2005.

Team Members

The team's current drivers are Max Verstappen and Sergio Perez.

Car

The team's car for the current season is the RB20.

History

Red Bull Racing has won multiple championships in Formula 1 and is known for its innovative designs and aggressive racing style.
